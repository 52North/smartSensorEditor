Identifikation

 

Die Identifikationswerte werden für Suchvorgänge (z.B. Discovery Profil) definiert.

 

The identifier property takes a Term as its value. The Term has a definition attribute that specifies in this case the type of identifier, while the codeSpace attribute specifies that the value of the identifier is according to the rules or enumerations of a particular authority. The identification properties should be considered as information suitable for the discovery applications.

 

Beispiel:
An identifier with a definition of “http://sensors.ws/def/tailNumber” might take “N291PV” as its value based on the codespace of a US Air Force rules dictionary. Other possible definitions for identifiers might include, for example, shortName, longName, acronym, missionID, processorID, serialNumber, manufacturerID, or partNumber.

 

XML Path:
/sml:PhysicalSystem/sml:identification/sml:IdentifierList/sml:identifier
